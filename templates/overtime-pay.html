Overtime Pay Investigation

Overtime in public-jobs is not a great practice. It is taxing for employees, expensive for taxpayers, and prone to abuse.
The next few visualizations will help bring clarity into overtime pay with deep-divess.


High level side-by-side bar chart of the departments with the highest total overtime pay from 2017 - 2021.


Bubble chart of average overtime pay (color), by department, sized by number of employees. Filter for the year(s) of interest.


Drilled down by County and grouped by Positions instead of Departments. This gives a more granular view of overtime pay versus positions within a specific county.


Most granular view of employees with job titles of interest with the highest number of work hours per week and highest overtime pay. Each data point is an employee. Employees with bigger sizes, darker circles, and further to the top right should be investigated.
