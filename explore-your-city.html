Frequently Asked Questions

  • How should I use this dashboard?

    Feel free to select your city and look at where employee compensation is going! You can look at pay types, pay by departments, and compare your city's pay to other cities'.

  • How do I select my city?

    Use the selector on the top right corner of the dashboard to select your primary city. Each tab of the dashboard will then hone into your primary city.

  • How do you select a year?

    Year filters are available for each tab of the dashboard where applicable. In Compensation Overview and Compare to Other Cities tabs, they are available on the top right corner. In Compensation by Department, year filter is available on the X-axis of each chart except for the bubble chart, of which the year filter is available on the top right corner.

  • What is the take-away from this dashboard?

    The primary take-away from this dashboard is to provide a tool for you to explore public employee compensation in your city. You can use this dashboard to compare your city's compensation to other cities' and to see how compensation is distributed across departments. Data Room tab is available where you can see 3 different deep-dive analyses on public employee compensation.
